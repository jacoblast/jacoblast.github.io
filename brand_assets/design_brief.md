# Project
Whole Tone Piano Works

A piano tuning and service practice focused on holistic instrument care, craftsmanship, and thoughtful personal service.

---

# Design Philosophy

The website should feel:

- calm
- refined
- intelligent
- trustworthy
- warm and human

Avoid:

- flashy design
- gradients
- bright colors
- trendy UI
- corporate aesthetics

The aesthetic should feel like:

- a craftsman's workshop
- a classical music publication
- a thoughtful artisan service

Think:

"quiet expertise".

---

# Visual Style

Minimalist design.

Lots of whitespace.

Typography-forward layout.

Content width should be narrow and readable.

700px
