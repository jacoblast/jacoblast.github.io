My Approach

A piano is an interconnected system.

There are a thousand small adjustments that can make a piano sing. I see each visit as an opportunity to improve the instrument as a whole: tuning, touch, and tone.

Equally important is the chance to connect with the people behind the piano — young and old — and share some of the knowledge and excitement I bring to every visit.
