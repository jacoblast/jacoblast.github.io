A Holistic Approach

Full-Spectrum Care

A piano is an interconnected system. Tuning, action, tone, and mechanical health all influence one another.

Full-spectrum care means that I pay attention to more than just tuning. During each visit, I assess:

  • Pitch (tuning)
  • Action calibration and touch consistency
  • Voicing (tonal quality and evenness)
  • Any mechanical issues that affect performance

The goal is to make the most meaningful overall improvement to your instrument within the time and budget available.

Not isolated perfection — but integrated refinement.

High-Tech Analog

Traditional Craft,
Modern Insight

My work is primarily aural. Listening is central.

At the same time, I bring a deep understanding of beat rates, harmonic structure, and string behavior. When appropriate, I use modern tools to enhance precision and efficiency.

Technology supports the ear — it does not replace it.

This balance allows for work that is both musically sensitive and structurally sound.
