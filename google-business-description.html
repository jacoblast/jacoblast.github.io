Business Profile Description

Whole Tone Piano Works offers piano tuning and service in the Vancouver–Portland area. Jacob Last is an aural-first piano craftsman, guided by a background in jazz performance, mathematics, and education — a combination that brings musical sensitivity, technical precision, and human warmth to every visit.

Each appointment begins with a full evaluation of the instrument before moving into tuning, touch, and tone. Services range from standard maintenance visits to extended regulation, voicing and repair work for instruments needing deeper attention.

Serving homes, studios, churches, and schools throughout Southwest Washington and Northwest Oregon.
